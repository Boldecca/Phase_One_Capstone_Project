About Book Explorer

Book Explorer helps readers discover millions of books from all over the world.

Powered by the Open Library API .

Built using JavaScript and Tailwind CSS.

📚 Whether you're a passionate reader or just exploring — Book Explorer is your gateway to stories.

Features

🔍

Search Books

Search through millions of books by title, author, or subject using the Open Library API.

❤️

Save Favorites

Keep track of books you love by adding them to your favorites list.

💾

Persistent Storage

Your favorites are saved locally and persist across browser sessions.

📚

Discover Books

Browse trending fiction books and explore new titles from around the world.
